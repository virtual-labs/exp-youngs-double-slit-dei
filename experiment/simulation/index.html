Double Slit Sand Simulation

Click Start Sand Flow to begin the animation. Show Graph displays the intensity distribution pattern!

📋 Procedure

  1. Start Sand Flow: Click the "Start Sand Flow" button to begin dropping sand particles through the double slits.
  2. Observe Particle Behavior: Watch as individual sand grains pass through either the left or right slit, behaving as classical particles.
  3. Notice the Pattern: As more particles accumulate, observe how they form two distinct piles behind each slit.
  4. Stop the Animation: Click "Stop Sand Flow" to pause the simulation and freeze the current state.
  5. View the Graph: After stopping, click "Show Graph" to see the intensity distribution showing two separate peaks.
  6. Classical Behavior: Notice that unlike waves, classical particles create two bands - no interference pattern occurs.
  7. Reset: Click "Reset Simulation" to clear the sand and start a new experiment.
